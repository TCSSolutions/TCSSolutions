About The California Sound Solutions

The California Sound Solutions is a family-owned and operated sound rental company with a legacy spanning three generations. Originally established in Sri Lanka, the business proudly served clients from 2010 to 2020, delivering high-quality audio solutions for a wide range of events. Now relaunching in California, the company brings its rich heritage and expertise to the vibrant event and entertainment industry in the United States.

Rooted in a tradition of excellence and innovation, The California Sound Solutions is committed to providing exceptional sound rental services, featuring state-of-the-art equipment and personalized technical support. The company prides itself on its dedication to creating seamless and memorable audio experiences, whether for corporate events, live performances, weddings, or private gatherings.
